ABOUT ViSALUS

ViSalus™ is a lifestyle transformation company that champions personal achievement through the Body by Vi™ Challenge, a 90-day health and fitness platform. ViSalus is committed to inspiring personal transformation and entrepreneurship through a social marketing model, global community culture and premium nutritional products.

ViSalus was founded in 2005 by Ryan Blair, Blake Mallen and Nick Sarnicola—three individuals passionate about helping people achieve the best in life, health and prosperity. Despite their varied backgrounds, all three men were driven, successful entrepreneurs by the time their paths crossed in 2004. They combined their complementary skill sets and embarked on a shared mission to inspire and empower people. Their vision for ViSalus was to transform personal wellness and mentor new entrepreneurs by challenging people to live up to their own greatness.

The concept of tailoring a personal challenge for mainstream America is what led to the creation of the Body by Vi Challenge. "Simple, Rewarding and Fun," the Body by Vi 90—Day Challenge quickly became the fastest growing weight—loss and fitness Challenge in North America, with more than 150,000 new people joining every month. Ryan, Blake and Nick have earned a reputation as progressive, social marketing pioneers. In a mere seven years, ViSalus has transformed the lives of millions of people with The Challenge and revolutionized the direct selling industry with a razor-sharp focus on innovation and technology. The forward-thinking company was the first to converge social media and network marketing, driving massive online engagement through the use of shareable content and cutting-edge digital tools such as Vi-Net® Mobile and Vi-Net Swipe.

Over the last two years, ViSalus has shattered sales records and experienced explosive growth. ViSalus was awarded the Direct Selling News' 2012 Bravo Growth Award for achieving over 600% revenue growth in 2011. In the first half of 2012, the company's momentum continued with record-breaking sales of $327 million. ViSalus debuted at #47 on the Direct Sales Industry Top 100 Global Companies and is rapidly climbing toward the top.

THE CHALLENGE

Today, ViSalus is the social marketing company synonymous with two words: The Challenge. The Body by Vi Challenge begins with a simple personal goal set by each individual to improve their physical health and have fun at the same time.

The Challenge is built on five unique Challenge Kits—The Fit Kit, Transformation Kit, Core Kit, Shape Kit and Balance Kit. Products in each Kit are scientifically designed to help burn fat, boost metabolism and control hunger. Whatever the personal goal, whether to lose weight, gain muscle or train for a 10k run—there’s a nutritional Challenge Kit to fit every lifestyle.

ViSalus encourages everyone to join the Challenge online and document their results along the way. At the end of each person’s 90-Day Challenge, they can submit their results for a chance to share in $25 million in free product, prizes and vacations every year.

Winners are selected based on the most inspiring transformation success stories and are crowned in six categories: Male, Female, Couple, Team, Family and a Random Drawing. Winners in each category are called Champions and they are announced and crowned three times per year at ViSalus National Events. Among other congratulatory gifts, Champions are ultimately rewarded with the Hollywood Transformation Vacation. These trips can include head-to-toe spa makeovers, stays at the best hotels, professional styling, new attire, photo shoots with celebrity photographers, sporting events, private jets, live theater productions and more, for a total star treatment!

New in 2013, The Body by Vi Challenge will now include three distinct platforms: Weight-Loss, Active and Fitness. This will allow anybody to join The Challenge and select a category to compete in depending upon whether they want to shed pounds, achieve their optimal physique or accomplish an athletic activity. Within each platform, there will still be the six categories of Champions and winners will be chosen based on 50% results, 25% personal story and 25% community vote.
