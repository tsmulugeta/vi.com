HOW TO START ENROLLING ON THE GO WITH VI-NET SWIPE (BETA)


Step 1
Upgrade your account to Vi-Net Swipe Here Your account will be upgraded from ViNet Pro ($29/month) to ViNet Swipe ($39/month). Rather than commissioning transactions, this $10 upgrade provides Promoters a flat fee that covers all banking and per transaction fees to accept swiped credit card payments.
wiper

Step 2
Download Vi-Net Mobile from mobile.visalus.com (link) or search in the Apple, BlackBerry or Android app store.

Step 3
Open the application on your Apple, Blackberry or Android device

Step 4
Click Enroll on the home screen to start enrolling a new Customer or Promoter. Select the Country of the Customer or Promoter you are enrolling, then the person's Challenge Kit of choice.

Step 5
Plug in the Swiper device Once you reach the enrollment form, plug in the Swiper device into your phone jack. You will be notified "Swiper Connected" when the device has paired.

Step 6
Swipe the Credit Card with the magnetic strip facing forward (the same direction as the Body by Vi logo on the device). The person's name, billing information and a suggested username will auto-populate. Please complete the empty fields and select the autoship date. Click Submit.

Step 7
Automatically Send Email Confirmation The newly enrolled Promoter or Customer will receive email confirmation with their new account information. Please advise them to check their junk mail box if the email is not received shortly.
